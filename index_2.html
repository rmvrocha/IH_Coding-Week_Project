About me

Why did you did Ironhack?

I've always been amazed at how things work. How a toy or tool can have many singular pieces working together. I began to dissect my toys and my father's work tools to satisfy my curiosity and the "why" of how they work.

I grew up to find that this is still my drive. So coming to Ironhack was a great connection to my childhood, my professional and academic experience. With UX/UI Bootcamp, I just created a point of intersection between my engineering and design background.

What do you look for in a company culture?

A company that seeks constant improvement, promotes teamwork and cooperation. It's important to ensure that the place has a nice work-life balance because with that is to grant the productivity and willing to grow of the employee.

Superpowers & Why?

Call me Digital MacGyver. Multidisciplinary knowledge that allow me to aproach problems by difference perpectives and finding the proper solution for user/team/company problem.
